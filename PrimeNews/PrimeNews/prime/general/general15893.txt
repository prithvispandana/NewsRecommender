Alan Shearer has been revealed to be one of the BBC's highest earners, netting between £400,000 and £449,999 per year from the license-payer-funded broadcaster. The former England captain's salary has come into question, with his role as a pundit on Match of the Day and other BBC shows seeing him paid significantly more than many of his contemporaries elsewhere in the corporation and particularly women, who are under-represented in the upper reaches of the Beeb's latest salary reveal.
