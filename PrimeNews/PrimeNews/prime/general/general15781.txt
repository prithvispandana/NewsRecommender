Deepika Padukone, who is on a career-high, courtesy her Bollywood and Hollywood projects, just added another feather to her hat. The actress has now been inducted as one of the newest members of the Academy of Motion Picture of Arts & Sciences (AMPAS), Class of 2017.  The 31-year-old, who made her Hollywood debut with Vin Diesel's 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage', took to Twitter to share her excitement. "Honoured to be a new #AcademyMemeber. @TheAcademy # Classof2017," Deepika tweeted.  Other Indian stars who have been invited to join the Academy and vote at the Oscars include, megastar Amitabh Bachchan, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Salman Khan, Priyanka Chopra and Irrfan Khan.
