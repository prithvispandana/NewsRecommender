The 1 per cent cap on public sector pay rises has been attacked by the Government’s own experts, who warned the ability to fill jobs “could deteriorate rapidly” The body which advises on salaries paid to some NHS managers, military officers and top civil servants warned it could not “operate effectively” with its hands tied so tightly by ministers. It questioned the “value in having an independent body” with the 1 per cent limit in place – also highlighting how staff are becoming “frustrated and demotivated”.
