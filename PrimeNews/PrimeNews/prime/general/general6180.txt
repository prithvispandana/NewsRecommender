Sensex and Nifty consolidated on early gains on Monday as both the indices ended on record highs gaining over 1 per cent each.The 30-share BSE Sensex closed at 31,715.64 up by 355.01 points( 1.13 per cent), while the broader 50-share NSE Nifty ended at 9,763.05, gaining 97.25 points (1.01 per cent).
