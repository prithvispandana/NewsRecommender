MINNEAPOLIS (AP) — The family of an Australian woman who was shot and killed by police in Minneapolis says they're trying to understand why it happened. In a statement released by Australia's Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade, the family of the woman — whom Minneapolis authorities haven't identified — said it's a difficult time. The Star Tribune ( http://strib.mn/2tZtSB2 ) identified the woman as Justine Damond, 40, from Sydney, Australia.
