Will Arun Jaitley heed Rahul Gandhi's demand for a "full rollback" of GST on wheelchairs and Braille typewriters, like he did last year with the Congress leader's suggestion that Braille paper be tax exempt?  That remains to be seen, of course, but on Monday, the Congress vice president again slammed the Centre for its "hasty" rollout of the Goods and Service Tax (GST).
