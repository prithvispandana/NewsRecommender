18 year old beauty model Skye Olivia Mitchell was out driving her Toyota Yaris, when she unfortunately crashed into an oncoming Ford Transit Van near the village of Bootle. At the time of the incident, Skye Olivia Mitchell was accompanied by her 18 year old friend Caitlin Lydia Huddleston and another local woman seated at the back. While Skye and Caitlin died at the scene the third woman was immediately flown by an air ambulance to Royal Preston Hospital. The 51 year old male driver of the van with whom they collided with was flown to Aintree University Hospital in Liverpool. According to reports, both remain in critical condition.
