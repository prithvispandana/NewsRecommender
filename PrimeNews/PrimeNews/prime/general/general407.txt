Shah Rukh Khan stepped foot in Bollywood in the year 1992 with a role in Rishi Kapoor and Divya Bharti starrer 'Deewana'. The actor transcended every other contemporary in the 90s, setting up an empire of sorts. Shah Rukh, who is fondly called the 'Baadshah of Bollywood' by his fans, was also blessed with the gift of gab. As he celebrates 25 years of being in Bollywood, we take a look at 25 witty quotes by the King Khan himself.
