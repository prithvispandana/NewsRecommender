Hull have handed promising winger Nick Rawsthorne a new two-year contract. The 21-year-old joined the Challenge Cup holders in the winter from Championship side Halifax on a 12-month deal and made his Super League debut against Leigh in May. Rawsthorne is currently on loan with Leigh and has also spent time at League 1 clubs York and Doncaster but remains in the long-term plans of Hull coach Lee Radford.
