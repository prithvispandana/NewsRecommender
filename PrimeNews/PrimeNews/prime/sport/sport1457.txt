Real Madrid have announced the signing of left-back Theo Hernandez from city rivals Atletico. The 19-year-old will be presented at the Bernabeu on Monday after completing a move for an undisclosed fee, reported to be around 26million euros. Hernandez has yet to play a first-team game for Atletico but impressed while on loan at Copa del Rey finalists Alaves last season. Real announced the France Under-20 international has signed a six-year deal with the LaLiga champions.
