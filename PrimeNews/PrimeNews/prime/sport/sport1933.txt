If you missed Sunday's Warm Up show with Max Rushden and Barry Glendenning, you missed one of the all-time great sporting anecdotes. Would it be the start of a beautiful relationship? Could they overcome the language barrier? All is revealed... You can hear The Warm Up show with Max Rushden every Sunday from 11am-2pm.
