There was once a time when Chelsea and Tottenham had a good relationship, as retrofootballblog.com explains. You have to go back 71 years to find an example, though. It involved Eddie Baily, the brilliant inside forward in Spurs' first league title triumph.  Baily was on Spurs' books as an amateur in 1939, but the Second World War happened and the exciting youngster was sent to Belgium, the Netherlands and Germany with the Royal Scots Fusiliers.
