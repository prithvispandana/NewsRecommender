While Liverpool have managed to add record signing Mohamed Salah to their ranks this summer it hasn’t been such smooth sailing elsewhere. Two of Jurgen Klopp’s top targets – Southampton’s Virgil van Dijk and RB Leipzig’s Naby Keita – have proven rather more elusive to bring to Anfield. The Saints accused Liverpool of illegally tapping up Van Dijk but, even though the club publicly announced they were no longer interested, a move is supposedly still on the cards.
