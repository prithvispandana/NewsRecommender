Kevin Pietersen has refused to rule out playing for South Africa in the 2019 World Cup. The 37-year-old batsman last played for England in January 2014, and he will be eligible to play for the Proteas from next year. And he admits it’s possible he cold represent the country of his birth in the world Cup in England in two years’ time. He told Drivetime: “I’ll play [Twenty20 cricket] in South Africa for the next two years, I’ll play in Australia for the next two years, I’ll play in Pakistan for the next two years.
