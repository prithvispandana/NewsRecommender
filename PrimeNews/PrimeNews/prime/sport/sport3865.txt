Arsenal will have to shell-out £80million to sign Monaco star Thomas Lemar, smashing their transfer fee record for the second time a month in the process.  Lemar is Arsene Wenger's No 1 target - after landing striker Alexandre Lacazette - by the Ligue 1 side are demanding the massive sum in the hope of warding off interest from the Gunners, according to the Mirror. Arsenal will have to release a number of players to fund the move as Wenger continues his summer reshuffle.
