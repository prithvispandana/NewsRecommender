Everton have signed Sandro Ramirez from Malaga on a four-year deal, the Premier League club have announced. The 21-year-old Spanish youth international becomes the Toffees' fouth signing of the summer, after the Merseyside club triggered the release clause in his contract. He has put pen to paper on a deal until the end of June 2021, and moves to Goodison Park after a successful season-long spell with Malaga following his rise through the Barcelona youth ranks.
