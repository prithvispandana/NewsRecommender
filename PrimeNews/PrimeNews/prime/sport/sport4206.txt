Fenerbahce have set their sights on out-of-favour Everton striker Oumar Niasse, according to reports in Turkey. The Senegal international, who spent the second-half of last season on loan at Hull City, is not expected to be part of Ronald Koeman's long-term plans at Goodison Park. And Turkish newspaper Fanatik are reporting that Fenerbahce are currently in talks with the 27-year-old over a proposed loan move.
