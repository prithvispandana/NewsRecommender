Liverpool have already broken their transfer record this summer, and could yet spend even more in pursuit of Naby Keita. Mo Salah arrived from Roma for a club record £37.9m, but the Reds are preparing to almost double that amount to land midfielder Keita from Bundesliga club RB Leipzig. The latest report from The Times' respected Northern Football Correspondent Paul Joyce reveals Liverpool boss Jurgen Klopp's determination to bring Keita to Anfield.
