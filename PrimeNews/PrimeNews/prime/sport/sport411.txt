Listen back to the latest Lions' Den podcast, as Russell Hargreaves brings you all the latest news from the tour of New Zealand. On the eve of the second Test against in All Blacks in Wellington, Russ was joined by The Sun's Jonny Fordham to preview the clash at the Westpac Stadium. Russ also heard from Lions loosehead prop Mako Vunipola, defence coach Andy Farrell, plus All Blacks captain Kieran Read and flanker Jerome Kaino.
