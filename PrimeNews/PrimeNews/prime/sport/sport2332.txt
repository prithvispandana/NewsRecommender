Brendan Rodgers has warned Celtic's rivals: We'll be even better this season! The Hoops went the entire domestic campaign unbeaten in the Northern Irishman's first year at the helm, winning the Premiership, Scottish Cup and League Cup. And, speaking exclusively to talkSPORT on Thursday, Rodgers said the signings the club have made this summer and the increased confidence in the camp has him convinced even greater things are around the corner.
