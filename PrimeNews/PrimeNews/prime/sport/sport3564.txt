Juventus have held talks with Chelsea and Manchester United target Kostas Manolas as they try to tie up a deal for the Roma defender. The Italian champions are currently on the hunt for a new centre-back after selling Leonardo Bonucci to AC Milan. Juve have reportedly now set their sights on Manloas, who has previously been linked with a move to Chelsea and Manchester United. Indeed, according to La Reppublica, Juventus general manager Beppe Marotta met with Roma sporting director Monchi over the weekend to try broker a deal.
