Manchester United goalkeeper Sam Johnstone has joined Aston Villa on a season-long loan. Johnstone, 24, returns to Villa having made 22 appearances for the Championship club following an initial loan switch from United in January. "I am delighted to be back," former England Under-20 international Johnstone told the official Villa website. "I really enjoyed my last spell here - it's a great club with top players and passionate fans. "I can't wait to get started now.
