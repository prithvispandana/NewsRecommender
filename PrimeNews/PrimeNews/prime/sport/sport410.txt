It appears Dani Alves is closing in on his move to Manchester City having left Juventus after just one season in Italy. The Brazil full-back looks set to link up with Pep Guardiola again, having enjoyed an extremely successful spell at Barcelona together. Should the deal go through this summer, Alves will be moving to the Premier League at the age of 34.
