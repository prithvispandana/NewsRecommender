Bournemouth midfielder Dan Gosling has signed a new four-year contract at the Premier League club. The 27-year-old, who joined the Cherries in May 2014, will remain at the south-coast outfit until at least the summer of 2021. Devon-born Gosling began his career at Plymouth before moving onto Everton and Newcastle. "I've really enjoyed my three years here," Gosling told the official Bournemouth website. "It's gone so fast but I believe I've improved immensely, working with the manager and his coaching staff.
