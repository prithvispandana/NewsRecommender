Yahoo's core internet business was acquired by Verizon in July 2016 for $4.8 billion. Many media houses have reported lack of strong leadership, failed attempts to acquire good companies and loosing precious opportunities to itself get acquired at a higher valuation as the possible reasons for the company's downfall. But these were just missed opportunities and rarely highlight the mindset of the people, the working environment, the inside processes that could have weakened the company in holding its ground as a tech giant.This is a guide to insider insights that shed light on Yahoo's brewing problems early on.
