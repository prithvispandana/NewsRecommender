Time travel is as little understood as it is popular. Pop culture has shown humans (and dogs) getting into a cabin (or car) like machine with dials and knobs that ship you through time in a few seconds. But is it really possible to travel through time or is it all just science fiction?This guide is an effort to explain how time travel to the future could really work from a layman's point of view.
