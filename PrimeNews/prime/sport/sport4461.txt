Who is the most valuable player under the age of 21? We are close to seeing the first £100m player and, a new study by Soccerex using a Football Value Index by sports marketing company Prime Time Sport, reveals the 20 players who are closest to costing the landmark fee. According to Soccerex, this Football Value Index is used by football clubs and the sport industry 'to obtain an independent and accurate valuation of football players.'
