New Huddersfield signing Tom Ince did not need any persuading to join head coach David Wagner's newly-promoted bandwagon. Former England Under-21 international Ince, 25, has signed a three-year deal after the Terriers agreed an undisclosed fee with Championship club Derby. "To be given the opportunity to be in the Premier League with a team that's on the up is a great feeling," Ince told Huddersfield Town TV.
