Mark Saggers was joined by a very special guest on Wednesday's Kick-Off show - tennis superstar Serena Williams. The 23-time Grand Slam singles winner spoke of her desire to return to the sport in 2018 following the birth of her first child. She also discussed her sister Venus' career resurgence and her semi-final clash with Britain's Johanna Konta at Wimbledon, her involvement with mattress maker Tempur and more!
