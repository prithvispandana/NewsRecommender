Chelsea confirmed their second signing of the summer transfer window on Sunday, adding Antonio Rudiger from Roma to Antonio Conte's squad. Willy Caballero has also been brought to Stamford Bridge on a free transfer, as the Italian manager builds his team for the 2017/18 campaign. However, they have been dealt a blow in the hunt for one of their key transfer targets, with Manchester United agreeing a deal to sign Everton's Romelu Lukaku subject to personal terms and a medical.
