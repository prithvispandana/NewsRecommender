Leicester have completed the signing of goalkeeper Eldin Jakupovic from Hull.  The 32-year-old has signed a three-year deal with the Foxes for a reported fee which could rise up to £2.25million.  He becomes the club's third summer signing and joins them in Hong Kong for the Premier League Asia Trophy, with Leicester facing West Brom on Wednesday.  Jakupovic told LCFC TV: "I'm very happy and excited that the deal is finally done. I'm very excited to be a part of something special here and I can't wait for the first game.
