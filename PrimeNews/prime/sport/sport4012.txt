Ahead of the new Premier League campaign, Arsenal have moved on from Australia and now play Bayern Munich in China in the next stage of their pre-season tour. Here's all the match info you need… Where is the match being played? Shanghai Stadium in Shanghai, China. What time is kick-off? The game will take place in China on Wednesday 19 July at 7.20pm local time. However, that means it is on at 12.20pm in the UK. What channel is it on? Fans can watch it on Premier Sports.
