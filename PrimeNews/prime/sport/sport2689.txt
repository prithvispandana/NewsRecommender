As teams splash the cash during the transfer window West Ham manager Slaven Bilic has had a fair bit to say on the matter. With astronomical levels of money now involved in football the Croat compared the situation the only way he knows how - by likening it to Justin Bieber and Elvis Presley. The former defender has always had a way with words and his most recent comments reminded us of some of his golden quotes. So read on to see our favourite Bilic lines starting with his most recent.
