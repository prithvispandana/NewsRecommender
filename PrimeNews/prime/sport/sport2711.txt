Phil Neville has backed former club Everton to break the top six stranglehold at the top of the Premier League having done the ‘best business’ of all clubs this summer. Speaking to talkSPORT’s Ian Abrahams at the Star Sixes event, Neville praised the Toffees’ work in the transfer market, which has seen them bring in eight players – five of whom should be guaranteed first-team starters.
