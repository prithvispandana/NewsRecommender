Everton have confirmed the signing of Nigeria international Henry Onyekuru from Belgian side Eupen - a deal which takes their summer spending past £50million. The 20-year-old, who finished the season as joint top-scorer in the Belgian Super League with 22 goals in 38 matches, has signed a five-year contract after a fee understood to be in the region of £7million was agreed earlier this week. However, he will immediately go out on loan to Anderlecht for next season where he will play in the Champions League.
