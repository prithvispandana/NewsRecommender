We are under a year away from the 2018 World Cup, and fans are starting to predict which players will be travelling to Russia for the tournament. The other side of that coin, however, is predicting the players set to miss out on being called up by their nations. One of the main reasons a player is not called up is bad performances and form at club level, and talkSPORT have collected a group of players who could miss out on Russia 2018 if they stay with their current side.
