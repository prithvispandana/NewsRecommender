Arsenal goalkeeper Wojciech Szczesny is set to join Juventus for £10m, according to reports in Italy. The Poland international has spent the past two seasons on loan at Roma and he is now set to leave permanently. Juventus have been pursuing the goalkeeper all summer and they appear to have finally got their man. According to Sky Italia, a deal has been agreed between Arsenal and Juventus for Szczesny and he will join them for £10m. The 27-year-old will reportedly fly to Italy in the coming days to finalise personal terms and undergo a medical.
