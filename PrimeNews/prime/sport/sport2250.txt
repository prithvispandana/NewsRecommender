Brighton have announced the signing of Austria left-back Markus Suttner from Ingolstadt. The 30-year-old has signed a three-year deal with the Seagulls having moved from the German second-tier side for an undisclosed fee. Brighton boss Chris Hughton told the club's website: "Markus is someone we have been aware of for some time, and I am pleased we have managed to complete the transfer at this stage.
