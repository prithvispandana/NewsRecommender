You put all that work in only for your rival to reap the reward – it must be annoying. With Dominic Solanke’s move to Liverpool, after spending his youth career at Chelsea, Jurgen Klopp will benefit from the player’s talents instead of the club that coached him. It is yet to be seen whether Solanke will be a success but he is by no means the only player to have left a club, where they were part of the youth system, that went on to shine for a direct challenger.
