Tottenham right-back Kyle Walker moved to Manchester City on Friday in a £50million transfer. The 27-year-old England international is the latest in a long line of big money deals to be completed by City since the 2008 takeover by Sheikh Mansour’s Abu Dhabi United Group. But, as is often touted, money doesn’t always buy you success no matter how much clubs splash the cash in the hope they will find the players to take them to the next level.
