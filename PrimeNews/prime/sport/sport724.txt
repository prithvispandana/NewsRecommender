Mauricio Pellegrino has made Poland Under-21 international defender Jan Bednarek his first signing as Southampton boss. Bednarek has joined the Premier League side from Lech Poznan for a fee of around £5million. The 21-year-old, who made 27 appearances for the Polish top-flight side last season, has penned a five-year contract.
