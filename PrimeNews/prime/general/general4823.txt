Authorities said a police officer was fatally shot early Wednesday as she sat in a command vehicle in New York.
48-year old Miosotis Familia was shot just after midnight while she and her partner were sitting in a marked police vehicle in the Bronx. The NYPD said she was taken to St. Barnabas Hospital, where she died.
Police are calling it an "unprovoked attack."
The suspect, Alexander Bonds, 34, allegedly drew a revolver as officers confronted him. Police Commissioner James O'Neill said he was shot and killed by police about a block away.
