A gunman who opened fire in a New York City hospital has been killed after shooting as many as a half a dozen people. The gunman reportedly began to open fire just after 3 p.m. local time, according to the New York City Police Department. The cause of his death is unclear. The hospital is on lock down as police respond to the incident. Reports indicate that at least three doctors are among those who have been shot. A total of four to six people may have been injured. Their conditions were not immediately known.
