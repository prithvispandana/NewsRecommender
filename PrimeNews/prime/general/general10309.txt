A 31-year-old man has been stabbed to death by two attackers on a moped during an early-morning altercation in south-east London, police say. The victim was fatally wounded during a clash in which shots were also fired in King William Walk, Greenwich, the Metropolitan Police said. He had first been approached by the pair at 12.15am on Saturday. The assailants, both male, then fled the scene on the moped and emergency services arrived to find the man with stab injuries.
