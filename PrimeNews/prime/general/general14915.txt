Fixing America’s failing health care system is one of the most pressing issues facing Congress and millions of Americans remain deeply worried over the issue — but that didn’t stop Democrats from openly celebrating a partisan victory Monday. Several leading Democrats reacted to news of the GOP’s third failure at repealing and replacing Obamacare with almost unrestrained glee. Sen.
