Mango lovers who find delight in Dussehri and can’t resist the charms of Chausa may soon savour the goodness of both in a single fruit. The Central Institute of Subtropical Horticulture has developed a hybrid of Lucknow’s Dussehri and Chausa varieties, which it says is a perfect mix of both. Named CISH-M-2, the hybrid ripens at the end of the Dussehri cycle and just before Chausa hits the market.
